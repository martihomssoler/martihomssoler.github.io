Hi! My name is Martí Homs Soler. I'm a Software Engineer, entrepeneur, tinkerer and writer.

This blog is my personal space, where I write mostly for myself to explain what I’m learning, clarify ideas, or document rabbit holes I fall into. You can find random thoughts and snippets of ideas under Blog.

You can find interesting tech stuff under Tech. It is mainly about personal software projects, NixOS, ergo-split keyboards and homelabbing, with the ocassional random note on here and there.
